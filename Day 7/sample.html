Learnings from Today's Session on Cloud Computing and AWS

Cloud Computing

  • Cloud computing is the on-demand delivery of IT resources over the internet with pay-as-you-go pricing.
  • Essential characteristics include on-demand self-service, broad network access, resource pooling, rapid elasticity, and measured service.
  • Deployment models include Private Cloud, Public Cloud, and Hybrid Cloud.
  • Service models include Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS), Platform as a Service (PaaS), and Software as a Service (SaaS).
